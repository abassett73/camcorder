# 🎬 Camcorder 2.0

A browser-based screen and audio recorder with live transcription. Record your screen, narrate what you're doing, and get a transcript you can use to create knowledge base articles.

## Features

- **Screen + Audio Recording** — Capture your screen and microphone simultaneously
- **Audio Only Mode** — Just record your narration without screen capture
- **Live Transcription** — See your words transcribed in real-time as you speak
- **Editable Filenames** — Rename files before downloading
- **Video with Audio** — Screen recordings include your narration audio
- **No Server Required** — Runs entirely in your browser, nothing is uploaded anywhere

## How to Use

1. Open the app: **[Launch Camcorder 2.0](https://abassett73.github.io/camcorder/)**
2. Choose **Audio Only** or **Screen + Audio** mode
3. Click **Start Recording**
4. If using Screen + Audio, select which tab/window/screen to share
5. Narrate your workflow as you go
6. Click **Stop Recording** when done
7. Review, rename, and download your files
8. Paste the transcript into ChatGPT to generate a polished KB article!

## Browser Requirements

- **Google Chrome** or **Microsoft Edge** (recommended)
- Microphone permission required
- Screen sharing permission required for Screen + Audio mode
- All processing happens locally in your browser — no data is sent to any server

## Tips

- Speak clearly and at a natural pace
- Use transition phrases like "next", "and then", "now let's" to help structure your narration
- The transcript works great as input for ChatGPT to create step-by-step KB articles
- You can re-record if you're not happy with a take

## Privacy

Everything runs 100% in your browser. No audio, video, or transcript data is ever sent to any server. Files are only saved when you click the download buttons.
